##### Basics of Maya 
make sure to turn on poly count, selective details & object details!
grid settings > change length and width to 1000 > gride lines to 10 > subdivisions to 1
if grid disappearing > change far clip plane to a larger scale 
create a person reference, 180 units/40units/40units, to allow for scaling objects better
change workspace to modelling standard 
walls, floors and structural pieces MUST snap to the grid
move tool > W
rotation > E
scale > R
recentre/snap to screen > F
align to grid > X
snap to vertices > V
Extrude > ctrl + E 
reactivate last tool used > G
bevel > ctrl + b
how to fix multiple verts > merge verts once selecting the verts are selected 
cloning an object > ctrl + d
